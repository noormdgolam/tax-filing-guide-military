Tax Filing Guide for Military Rank Profile 5

Published: July 15, 2026 • Written by Editorial Team

Tax Benefits and Exemptions

Officers under Rank Profile 5 are entitled to a few tax benefits, including:

  • The Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC) may apply, depending on the officer's family status and income level.
  • A possible deduction for moving expenses when relocating as part of military duty, with some requirements and restrictions.

Tax Filing Requirements

Military personnel with a Rank Profile 5 are required to file Form 1040, which is the standard personal income tax return, to report their earned income. In addition to Form 1040, they must file:

  • Form W-2, which is provided by their employer (the military) and reports their wages and withholdings for the tax year.
  • Form 1095-A, B, or C, to report their health insurance coverage, as required by the Affordable Care Act (ACA) and the Consolidated Appropriations Act (CAA).
  • Form 4852, which is used to report and deduct any moving expenses incurred as part of their military service.

How to File Taxes

Gather all necessary tax documents and forms as mentioned above. You can choose between two primary filing options:

  • Submit your own paper return by mail or electronically through the Internal Revenue Service's (IRS) e-file process.
  • Take advantage of free or low-cost tax filing services provided by commercial tax software, the IRS, or military organizations.

Before preparing the tax return: ensure all income information from the W-2 form is correctly calculated, verify and adjust any deductions, including moving expenses when appropriate, and make any other needed adjustments. Extension Requests and Amended Returns

In cases where an extension on the April 15th tax filing deadline is required, or errors occur during the filing process, Rank Profile 5 personnel can:

  • File Form 4868 to claim a three-month extension to the tax filing deadline.
  • File Form 1040X, the Amended U.S. Individual Income Tax Return, to correct errors or omissions.
